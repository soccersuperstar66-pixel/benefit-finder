About GovBenefits

A free tool to help you find the benefits you deserve.

What is GovBenefits?

GovBenefits is a free, no-account-required eligibility estimation tool. Answer a few questions about your household size and income, and we'll instantly check whether you may qualify for up to 10 federal benefit programs — including food assistance, health coverage, energy help, and more.

We built this tool because navigating the U.S. benefits system is confusing. Many people who qualify for help don't apply simply because they don't know they're eligible or don't know where to start. GovBenefits aims to close that gap.

How We Calculate Eligibility

All calculations use 2024 Federal Poverty Level (FPL) guidelines published by the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services. Each program has its own income threshold as a percentage of FPL — for example, SNAP is ≤ 130% FPL, while WIC is ≤ 185% FPL.

These are estimates only. Actual eligibility is determined by your state agency and depends on factors we don't collect, including assets, immigration status, state-specific rules, and more. Always contact your local benefits office for an official determination.

For official eligibility determinations, contact your state's Department of Social Services or visit Benefits.gov.
